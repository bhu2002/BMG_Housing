About Surya Valencia

Surya Valencia is a Spanish-inspired gated villa community by Surya Developers, set along Doddaballapur Main Road in Yelahanka, North Bangalore. Spread across roughly 9 to 9.5 acres, the project brings together 122 independent villas designed for buyers who want space, privacy, and a resort-style setting without leaving the city behind. With low-density layouts, generous plot sizes, and a Mediterranean architectural theme, Surya Valencia is positioned as a long-term home as much as it is an investment in North Bangalore's growth corridor.

The development is being delivered in two phases, giving buyers flexibility on possession timelines depending on the villa and phase they choose.
