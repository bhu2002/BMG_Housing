About Echoes of Eden

Echoes of Eden is a low-density villament development by Sumo Group, located on Navarathna Agrahara Road near Devanahalli and Airport Road in North Bangalore. Spread across 2 acres with just 57 exclusive residences across three towers, the project blends the independence of villa living with the convenience of an apartment community — ground and first-floor homes come with private backyard gardens and pools, mid-level residences are built around internal courtyards, and the top floor is reserved for single-level sky villas and penthouses with elevated views.

With only 8 villaments per floor and imported fittings throughout, the project is positioned toward buyers who want a premium, exclusive address close to the airport corridor and North Bangalore's growing business hubs.
